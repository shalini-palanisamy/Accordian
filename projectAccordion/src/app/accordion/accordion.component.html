IDP Education is an international education organization that helps students, from around the world, pursue their higher education goals. The company operates in the education and migration services sector, facilitating the process for students who wish to study abroad.

Student Placement Services: IDP assists students in finding suitable study programs and institutions abroad based on their academic background, interests, and career goals. IELTS Testing: IDP is a co-owner of the International English Language Testing System (IELTS), a widely recognized English language proficiency test. They offer IELTS testing services globally.

IELTS, or the International English Language Testing System, is a standardized test designed to assess the language proficiency of individuals who want to study or work in environments where English is used as the language of communication. IELTS is widely recognized and accepted by universities, employers, and immigration authorities in many English-speaking countries.

IELTS, or the International English Language Testing System, is a standardized test designed to assess the language proficiency of individuals who want to study, work, or migrate to countries where English is used as the language of communication. Here is some key information about IELTS: Test Components: Listening: Assessing the ability to understand spoken English in various contexts. Reading: Evaluating reading skills through a range of texts and exercises. Writing: Testing the ability to express ideas in a written form. Speaking: Assessing oral communication skills through face-to-face interviews.
